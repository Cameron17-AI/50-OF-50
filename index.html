What is 50 of 50?

CAN YOU FINISH

A fitness endurance competition. Compete globally via an automated Challenge runner. Automatic timing and automatic movement sequence. No machines, no weights, no shortcuts. A brutal high-rep exercise test. You pace yourself, complete every rep, and see if you can finish strong. If at all!

  • 50 reps per exercise
  • 50 unique exercises
  • Order is locked
  • Rest allowed at any time
Equipment
Open floor space + one bench/chair
Surface
Flat ground
Goal
Finish the full list in your best time.
Train it now
Practice the sequence. Improve your time.

Challenge FAQ

Quick answers for first-time competitors and anyone preparing for an official attempt.

  • What is 50 of 50? A timed fitness challenge with 50 exercises for 50 reps in a locked order.
  • Can you rest? Yes. Rest is allowed at any time, but the clock keeps running.
  • What equipment do you need? Flat ground and one bench or chair.
  • Where should you start? Use the demo first, then review the movements and rules before an official attempt.
